# MiCasa — Restaurant Management App

## Product Overview
MiCasa is a restaurant management app built for restaurant managers and floor staff. It replaces chaotic WhatsApp-based scheduling and scattered reservation tracking with a single, reliable, mobile-first platform.

## Users
- **Primary**: Restaurant managers — build rotas, assign shifts, push updates to staff
- **Secondary**: Floor staff — view their schedule, confirm shifts, receive instant updates

## Core Problem
Three interconnected pain points:
1. **Staff scheduling chaos** — shifts managed over WhatsApp, no single source of truth
2. **Reservation chaos** — no unified reservation view
3. **Inventory blind spots** — no tracking or alerts

## MVP Focus: Staff Scheduling
The MVP solves scheduling first. Managers can:
- Build and edit weekly rotas
- Assign shifts to specific staff members
- Push instant schedule updates with notifications
- Get confirmation from staff

Floor staff can:
- View their upcoming shifts in a clean, mobile-friendly schedule
- Confirm or flag issues with their assigned shifts

## Secondary Features (Post-MVP)
- Reservation board — unified view of all bookings
- Inventory tracking — stock levels and alerts

## Brand & Tone
- **Tone**: Practical, warm, reliable
- **Mobile-first**: Optimised for phones used on the floor
- **Simplicity**: Minimal training required — staff should understand it in minutes
- **Name**: MiCasa (evokes hospitality, ownership, home)
