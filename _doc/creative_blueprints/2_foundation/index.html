Why It Exists

The gap between a busy restaurant and a well-run one.

The Problem

Three critical operations — each managed in a different place, none of them talking to each other.

01

Walk-in & reservation chaos

No single view of bookings, covers, and wait times

02

Staff coordination friction

Shift changes live in group chats; no one has the latest schedule

03

Inventory blind spots

Manual counts, surprise stock-outs, and avoidable waste

The Solution

A single operational hub where the owner, managers, and floor staff all see the same picture — in real time.

✓

Live reservation & walk-in board

Every booking, cover count, and wait time in one view

✓

Staff scheduling & instant updates

Shifts confirmed and communicated without the group chat

✓

Smart inventory tracking

Real-time stock levels with automatic low-inventory alerts

One app. Every role. The whole operation — visible at once.
